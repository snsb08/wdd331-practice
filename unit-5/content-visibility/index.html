content-visibility Demo

This page has 40 cards in the markup. Build out css/main.css so they stack in a single column with comfortable spacing and the page scrolls well past the viewport. Then open Chrome DevTools, go to More Tools > Rendering, enable Paint Flashing, and compare scrolling before and after you add content-visibility: auto and contain-intrinsic-size to .card.
